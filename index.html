Why Scout is sunsetting

Sentinel Scout did its job. It proved that ordinary people, given the right tooling and a reason to care, will go out and find, test, and vouch for the nodes that make decentralized, uncensorable bandwidth real.

That mission is now graduating into Runner — the program that takes everything Scout learned and operationalizes it: from reconnaissance to sustained operation, from scouting nodes to running the network.

The way we close Scout is a debt of gratitude made concrete. No contribution is forgotten. No data is destroyed — it is archived, and this page is its public face.

Scout found the way. Runner walks it.

Every Scout has a standing invitation into Runner. Your history carries forward — this is a door, not a wall.
